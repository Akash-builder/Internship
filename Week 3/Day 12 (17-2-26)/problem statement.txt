Problem Statement

You are given raw e-commerce order data containing customer and product details.
Your task is to:

1. Remove duplicate orders (based on order_id)
2. Fix gender values (m → male, f → female)
3. Handle invalid age values
4. Fill missing values in city column using mode
5. Standardize product category names
6. Convert order date to datetime format
7. Extract month names
8. Extract day names
9. Rename columns to lowercase
10. Perform business analysis:
    • Total revenue
    • Top cities by sales
    • Category-wise sales
